Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2025

This web application, The Honeymaker, uses GitHub and Google OAuth for authentication. When you sign in, we receive basic profile information (such as your email and display name) from the provider you choose.

We do not share or sell your data. The data is used solely to manage your game profile, store high scores, and personalize your experience within the app.

All data is stored securely in a backend database hosted on Render, and is not accessible to anyone outside the development team.

If you have any questions or want your data removed, feel free to contact the developer .
